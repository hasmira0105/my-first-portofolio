Exam Scores Dashboard based on student's characteristics

I designed a student dashboard using Excel tools like PivotTable, PivotChart, Slicers, and Macros. The dataset was originally pulled from Kaggle, consists of 1000 records with 5 student attributes (gender, ethnicity, parents' educational levels, lunch, and test preparation) and exam scores in 3 subjects: mathematics, reading, and writing.

Assume that as a data analyst, we were requested to identify distinct patterns in average exam results based on student characteristics. In light of this, we constructed the dashboard shown below:

Here's what we can discover of each chart:

  1. In the first chart (top-left), we grouped students by ethnicity and plotted the average result of 3 exams. We observe that students from group A have the highest average scores, whereas the students from group B have the lowest. If you wish to compare students' average scores of a particular ethnicity, use the slicer on the left side of the chart
  2. The second chart (preparation course), shows that students who complete the preparation course have higher average scores.
  3. According to the parents' educational level chart, Students whose parents have finished a master's degree have the highest scores, whereas students whose parents have a high school education earn the lowest scores.
  4. Based on the Lunch chart, students who have the standard lunch are more likely to have a higher average exam results than students who have reduced/free lunch
